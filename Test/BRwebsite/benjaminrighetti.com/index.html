Hello There! I am Naomie L'Archeveque-Carriere. I am a student in Computer Engineer based in Sherbrooke (Canada). I like to call myself an Artist and Web Designer who loves creativity, a Technology Enthousiast who always have big projects in mind, a Big Reader who is always trying to learn new things, and a Globe Trotter who is always seeking new adventures.

I write articles on my blog page and share my life on social media. I love the nature, mostly the sky and its clouds and stars. I grew up in a small village surrounded by mountains, lakes and the stars, and I try to balance my varied activities with rigor and joy.
